Main Info

One night in 2018, without a seat, Smudge stole one from a salad-eating guest. Claiming his place at the table, he wasn’t impressed with what was on the plate. In the now-famous image, Smudge -- ears tense and mouth ajar -- appears at once confused, revolted and dejected about the greens in front of him. “He only eats meat so I would have to say that he’s always hated vegetables,” said Stillabower. The image was originally posted to Tumblr in 2018, but became an internet sensation when a Twitter user in May combined it with a screenshot from a 2011 episode of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills to become the “Woman Yelling at a Cat” meme. Both the images of Taylor Armstrong and Smudge were already widely used as reaction memes online, but together they became one of the most popular jokes on the internet.
